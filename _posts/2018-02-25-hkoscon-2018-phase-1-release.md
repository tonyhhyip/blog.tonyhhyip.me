## Offline Website

Last year, Team 404 Busters has tried to add the progressive web app into the website in order to provide the schedule offline. However, it is not successfully done. In this year, it is my honour to announce that we have successfully made it! You can browse it offline after finishing your first loading. It make use of [sw-precache](https://github.com/GoogleChromeLabs/sw-precache) to generate the Service Worker to provide the offline. Of course, support of Service Worker is required for this wonderful feature. Chrome 45 or above and Firefox 32 or above, as well as the Android Chrome can support it well.

> Note: for iOS device, iOS Safari 11.3 which is not yet the main stream, may be able to support this feature.
